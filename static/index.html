Get help understanding your cancer

Five trusted helpers will research your situation using public medical sources and put together a plain-language summary you can bring to your next appointment.

How this works

  1. You share. A few details about your cancer, your location, and your language.
  2. Five helpers research. A movement specialist, a dietician, a speech-and-swallowing specialist (when relevant), an emotional wellbeing specialist, and a patient navigator search trusted public sources.
  3. You get a summary. Plain language, in your language, with sources you can check. Bring it to your next appointment.
